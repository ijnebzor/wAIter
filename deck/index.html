THE PROBLEM

Established hospo runs on duct-taped tools.

  • Operators run 4–7 disconnected systems: POS, roster, inventory, bookings, accounting, supplier email.

03 / 12 · THE INSIGHT
THE INSIGHT

Incumbents won't switch. They'll add.

The market is full of replacement plays — *new* POS, *new* roster app, *new* inventory tool. Each demands a forklift change, and most established operators say no.

wAIter is the layer that runs alongside the existing stack first. Mirrors the data, surfaces what the tools hide, earns trust. Then absorbs categories one at a time, on the operator's clock — not ours.
